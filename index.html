Quick Help

  • H - Hallway Tool: Click to start, click again to end a hallway
  • O - Orthogonal Hallway Tool: Click to start, click again to end an orthogonal hallway with 90° bends
  • P - Room Tool: Click to add vertices, close the polygon to create a room
  • R - Rectangular Room Tool: Click to create corner, click again to create opposite corner
  • M - Move Tool: Hover to select, then click and drag to move. You can select polygons, edges, or individual vertices.
  • D - Delete Tool: Click on hallways or rooms to delete them
  • Alt/Opt + Click: Delete mode (temporary)
  • Shift + Drag: Pan the view
  • Scroll: Zoom in/out
  • Ctrl+Z: Undo
  • Ctrl+Y: Redo
  • Escape: Cancel current action
